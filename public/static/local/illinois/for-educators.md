# Resources for Educators

## IDHH

Search the IDHH for educational resources by [historical topics, notable Illinoisans, locations, or by format](/). Visit the [collection highlights blog](http://finditillinois.org/idhh/) for inspiration for your own research and writing. [Try creating your own item sets](/lists). For information on using and citing IDHH materials, see the [Copyright and Usage Guide](/about/usage-terms). More subjects, community histories, notable people, primary source sets, and exhibits created from Illinois Digital Heritage Hub collections are planned for future release.
